Premium Protection

Paint That Shines For Years

Ceramic coating is the pinnacle of paint protection. Unlike traditional waxes that wash away in weeks, our professional-grade nano-ceramic coating chemically bonds to your vehicle's paint surface, creating a permanent layer of protection that repels water, dirt, UV rays, bird droppings, tree sap, and chemical contaminants.

The result? A hydrophobic, glass-like finish that makes your car effortlessly easy to clean and keeps it looking like it just rolled off the showroom floor — for years, not weeks. Your car will have a depth of shine and clarity that you've never seen before.

Every ceramic coating application begins with a full decontamination wash, paint correction to remove any existing defects, and then meticulous ceramic coating application. We document every step of the process so you can see the transformation.

What's Included

  • Pre-treatment decontamination wash
  • Clay bar paint decontamination
  • Paint correction (single-stage polish)
  • IPA wipe-down & panel prep
  • Professional ceramic coating application
  • Infra-red curing (if applicable)
  • All paint panels, trim & glass
  • Wheel ceramic coating option
  • Full post-service inspection
  • Care & maintenance instructions
