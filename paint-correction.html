Paint Correction

A Flawless Mirror Finish

Over time, your car's paint accumulates a range of defects — swirl marks from improper washing, light scratches from bushes and keys, water spots from minerals, and oxidation from UV exposure. Paint correction is the professional process of removing these defects permanently, restoring your paint to a flawless, like-new condition.

We use professional dual-action and rotary polishing machines paired with high-grade compounds and polishes to safely remove defects from your paint's clear coat. The process is meticulous and time-intensive — but the results are nothing short of transformational. Dark colored vehicles especially see a jaw-dropping improvement.

Paint correction is also the mandatory first step before any ceramic coating application, ensuring the coating bonds to a perfect surface. We offer single-stage, two-stage, and multi-stage paint correction depending on the severity of your paint's condition.

01
PAINT INSPECTION
Full paint assessment using LED inspection lights to identify all defects
02
MACHINE POLISH
Compound and polish stages to safely remove defects from clear coat
03
FINAL FINISH
Panel wipe and finishing polish for a mirror-like, defect-free result
